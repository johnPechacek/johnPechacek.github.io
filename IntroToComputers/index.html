The Problem

How might we improve the accuracy of Istation's reading assessment data gathered from our youngest students?

The project began as a response to conversations the Istation Spanish team had with PreK and Kindergarten teachers. We wanted to know more about their experience using Istation with such young students.

These teachers were frustrated. They told us that the Istation assessments was the first time many of their students ever interracted with a computer. The assessments assume that all students are capable of performing basic actions with a mouse, but using a mouse can be a challenging motor skill to learn for 4-6 year olds. The teachers we talked to were having to help their students individually during assessments, and ultimately the data we were collecting from this age group was very inacurrate.
